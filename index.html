Our Mission

Genesis Revelation Inc exists to advance the gospel of Jesus Christ and to faithfully steward and share the knowledge, wisdom, and truth of God as revealed in Scripture—from Genesis to Revelation.

This ministry serves the Church by providing Scripture-centered teaching, study resources, and biblical clarity for the glory of God alone.

Genesis Revelation Inc is a non-profit organization. All content is offered freely as a gift. Donations are never required, but are sincerely appreciated as they help support the time, resources, and growth of this ministry.
