Our Mission

Soli Deo Gloria. Genesis Revelation Inc exists for the glory of God alone.

Our mission is to advance the gospel of Jesus Christ and to faithfully steward, preserve, and share the knowledge, wisdom, and truth of God as revealed in Scripture — from Genesis to Revelation.

This ministry is committed to serving the Church by providing Scripture-centered teaching, study resources, and biblical clarity that remain fully aligned with the Word of God. Our aim is to strengthen believers, encourage faithfulness, and point all people to Christ.

Everything produced through Genesis Revelation Inc is offered freely as a gift, trusting that God Himself supplies what is needed for the growth and nourishment of His people.
